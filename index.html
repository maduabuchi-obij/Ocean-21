We are doing more than you expect

Here, at Ocean 21, in the center of Lagos, we don't accept compromises. Not about our food or drinks. All our ingredients are sourced from local producers to ensure quality and freshness.

That's why our menu is seasonal while offering multi cuisine dishes, creative cocktails and their special crafted beers. Today, we may serve you Crispy calamary and prawns, and tomorrow we may have a Spicy Asun on the menu. But the taste is always the same: finger-licking good!

Don't hesitate to pay us a visit if you share our passion for fresh dishes cooked with only the best ingredients. We promise an experience that will keep you coming back for more!

We are located in the heart of Lagos

We are located in the heart of Lagos, a vibrant and bustling city known for its rich culture, dynamic energy, and diverse culinary scene. Situated amidst the lively streets and thriving community, our establishment offers convenience and accessibility, making it the perfect destination for locals and visitors alike to experience the essence of Lagos.
